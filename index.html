Function of Thiviya Builders (M) Sdn. Bhd. as a Project Management Consultant for Umbai, Melaka Reclamation Projects

As the Project Management Consultant (PMC) for the Umbai, Melaka Reclamation Projects, Thiviya Builders (M) Sdn. Bhd. is responsible for overseeing the successful planning, execution, and completion of the entire development in line with regulatory, environmental, and commercial objectives. Their key functions include:

1. Project Planning & Coordination
  • Develop detailed project execution plans and timelines.
  • Coordinate between government agencies, stakeholders, contractors, and consultants.
  • Ensure alignment with state and federal land reclamation and development policies.

2. Feasibility & Technical Oversight
  • Assist in the preparation and review of feasibility studies, including environmental impact assessments (EIA), hydrology, and soil investigations.
  • Provide expert recommendations on engineering design and marine works.

3. Regulatory Compliance
  • Facilitate applications and secure necessary approvals from authorities such as PLANMalaysia, DOE (Department of Environment), and Jabatan Laut.
  • Ensure all reclamation and development activities comply with Malaysian environmental and maritime laws.

4. Procurement & Contract Administration
  • Advise on procurement strategy and tender management.
  • Evaluate bids and assist in the selection of qualified contractors and consultants.
  • Administer contracts to ensure adherence to scope, budget, and quality standards.

5. Construction Supervision & Quality Control
  • Monitor construction activities for compliance with design specifications and safety regulations.
  • Conduct regular site inspections and report progress to stakeholders.
  • Implement quality assurance/control procedures throughout the reclamation and development process.

6. Stakeholder Engagement & Reporting
  • Liaise with local communities, investors, and government agencies.
  • Provide regular progress reports, financial updates, and risk assessments to the project owner/investor consortium.

7. Risk Management
  • Identify potential risks related to environmental, technical, or financial aspects.
  • Develop mitigation strategies and contingency plans.

Thiviya Builders (M) Sdn. Bhd.'s expertise ensures that the Umbai Melaka Reclamation Projects are executed efficiently, sustainably, and within the strategic objectives of turning Umbai into a key maritime, commercial, and industrial hub under the Melaka Free Zone initiative.

KEY HIGHLIGHTS
OF THE PROJECT

1. STRATEGIC LOCATION

  • Situated at Umbai, Melaka, directly on the Straits of Malacca, through which 80% of global oil trade passes annually.
  • Access to key international markets in Asia-Pacific, the Middle East, and beyond.

2. COMPREHENSIVE INFRASTRUCTURE

  • Development of state-of-the-art oil and petroleum storage facilities with scalable capacity.
  • Cutting-edge design for efficient storage, handling, and distribution of crude oil and refined petroleum products

3. WORLD-CLASS VISION

  • Establishing Melaka as a regional hub for oil storage, blending, and transshipment.
  • Supporting Malaysia’s aspirations to be a global leader in oil and gas logistics.

4. MARKET POTENTIAL

  • Unprecedented growth in energy demand across Asia-Pacific.
  • Opportunities to serve energy-importing giants like China, India, and Japan.
