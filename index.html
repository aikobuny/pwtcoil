KEY HIGHLIGHTS
OF THE PROJECT

1. STRATEGIC LOCATION

  • Situated at Umbai, Melaka, directly on the Straits of Malacca, through which 80% of global oil trade passes annually.
  • Access to key international markets in Asia-Pacific, the Middle East, and beyond.

2. COMPREHENSIVE INFRASTRUCTURE

  • Development of state-of-the-art oil and petroleum storage facilities with scalable capacity.
  • Cutting-edge design for efficient storage, handling, and distribution of crude oil and refined petroleum products

3. WORLD-CLASS VISION

  • Establishing Melaka as a regional hub for oil storage, blending, and transshipment.
  • Supporting Malaysia’s aspirations to be a global leader in oil and gas logistics.

4. MARKET POTENTIAL

  • Unprecedented growth in energy demand across Asia-Pacific.
  • Opportunities to serve energy-importing giants like China, India, and Japan.
